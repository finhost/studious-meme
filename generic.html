About Us

Who is Simplex, and what do they do?

Well, good question! Simplex is currently a group of 2 people. They are called m3ntal (Admin, builder, scripter, GFX) and Th3Vu1tur3 (Owner, builder, scripter, GFX).

We make Roblox games. Our Roblox games are stylish, fun, and intuitive. We have builders and scripters creating stuff whenever they can. We build Modern (fair difficulty), Industrial (easy), Sci-Fi (hard), Low Poly (fair), and others.

Let's talk about pricing. Sci-Fi style builds: R$50 per builder (R$100 in total). Industrial style builds: Free. Low Poly: Depends on the project. Can range from Free to R$100 per builder. Modern: Depends on the project. Can range from R$10 to R$250 per builder. If you want other styles, please get in touch with the owner.
